Chicken with Artichokes and Mushrooms

Our delicious chicken with artichokes and mushrooms!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 (14 oz.) can marinated quartered artichoke hearts, drained, liquid reserved
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1 tablespoon capers

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil and butter in large skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken in oil and butter for about 5-7 minutes per side; remove from skillet and set aside.
  2. Place artichoke hearts and mushrooms in the skillet and saute until mushrooms are brown and tender. Return chicken to skillet and pour in reserved artichoke liquid and wine. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for about 10-15 minutes, until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
  3. Stir in capers and simmer for another 5 minutes. Remove from heat and serve.
